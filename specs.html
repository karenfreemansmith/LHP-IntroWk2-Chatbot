Chatbot AI

This is a single page application that allows the user to talk to the webpage.

Specs

  • The AI portion of Chatbot will evolve to include more advanced conversational skills:
    • General greetings: hello/goodbye, how are you?, what's your name, etc.
    • Hobbies and Interests: have some she likes and others she doesn't.
    • Give her some common sense "wisdom" by having her repeat favorite quotes to questions.
    • Give her some religious opinions.
    • Give her some political opinions.
    • Give her a short history/bio - name, location, age, ethnicity, family, etc.
  • Chatbot's skills should evolve and adapt over time to the people she talks to:
    • Chatbot's facts come with a degree of certainty
    • "God" (the programmer) can give her 1000 certainty of her facts
    • Trusted people can give her a new fact of 100 certainty
    • Untrusted people can give her a new fact of 1 certainty
    • When people disagree with Chatbot it can introduce doubt. (certainty-1/certainty-100)
    • When people agree with Chatbot it can increase certainty. (certainty+1/certainty+100)
    • People earn trust by having things in common with Chatbot and lose it by disagreement.
    • People start at about 10 trust and can increase up to a max of 100 or a min of -100. This number of trust is how much certainty is added/subtracted to facts.
  • I may want to give Chatbot a few distinct personalities (different gender/cultural backgrounds/areas of knowledge):
    • The personalities can express themselves with preferred "speech ticks" - what greeting they use most, or what slang phrases/quotes they prefer
  • I want a cute cartoon-like UI, lips, robots, hotpink on light-gray...
  • Most of the page should be for the conversation, and it needs to be easy to read.
  • Speech bubbles would be nice, should resemble IM/chat on a mobile device
